About Me

Transplant to San Diego via Portland, Oregon and loving my life down here thus far. I studied Anthropology and International Studies at Portland State University before making the move to San Diego in 2015. I spent the last year or so going the nursing school route at Mesa College before deciding that this path was not really for me. After much debate and consideration, I decided to completely change paths and switch into unknown territory, web development. I look forward to gaining the skills and knowledge needed to be successful in this new career. In my free time, I enjoy beach days and hiking. I try to go on a hike at least once a week. I'm a true Oregonian and nature has always be very near and dear to my heart. In addition, I love world travel and have spent much time in South America over the years. I'm planning a big trip to The Philippines early next year and am super excited to explore a little slice of Southeast Asia.
